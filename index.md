### About Mother Teresa Women’s University (MTWU)

Mother Teresa Women’s University (MTWU), Kodaikanal, established in 1984 by the Government of Tamil Nadu, is a state university committed to academic excellence, research innovation, and women’s empowerment. Located in the serene hill station of Kodaikanal, MTWU promotes multidisciplinary education, research-led learning, and technology-enabled academic initiatives, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and the integration of emerging technologies to address regional, national, and global challenges.

MTWU offers a wide range of programs across Science, Mathematics, Management, Social Sciences, and Humanities, including:
- Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Research Programs in Science and Arts disciplines
- Doctoral Programs (Ph.D.) across multiple disciplines
- Professional and Technology-enabled courses, including AI, Data Science, and Intelligent Computing

The university emphasizes research, innovation, and capacity building, particularly empowering women scholars, researchers, and students. It supports advanced research through Funded research projects in UGC, DST, ICMR, ICSSR, IKS, CMRG, TANSCHE, NABARD etc and academic collaborations with various international and national institutions and industries
- Conferences, workshops, and faculty development programs
- Partnerships with national and international institutions

MTWU nurtures critical thinking, ethical values, leadership, and social responsibility, while advancing innovation in artificial intelligence, data science, intelligent computing, environmental studies, and applied sciences. Through outreach initiatives and community engagement, the university contributes significantly to societal transformation and knowledge dissemination.
