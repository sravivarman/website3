Submission

Submission

Use this page to understand how to submit, how submissions are reviewed, and the standards expected of authors.

Submission overview

Authors should prepare their materials according to the conference guidelines and submit through the official submission process.

Please ensure the submission matches the conference scope and formatting requirements before uploading.

Review process

Scope and format

Initial screening

Submissions are checked for relevance, completeness, and compliance with the required format.

Evaluation

Peer review

Qualified reviewers assess the submission for originality, clarity, and contribution.

Outcome

Decision and revisions

Authors receive a decision with any revision requests or acceptance details.

Ethics and Malpractice Statement

Author responsibility

Originality

Submitted work should be original and properly cite prior research and reused material.

Contribution

Authorship

All listed authors should have made a meaningful contribution to the work.

Publication standards

Integrity

Plagiarism, fabrication, falsification, and duplicate submission are not acceptable.
