Featured speakers

Opening Keynote

Dr. Maya Rodriguez

National Renewable Energy Laboratory

When EVs Become Grid Assets

Dr. Rodriguez studies managed charging, distribution constraints, and the systems-level effects of large-scale EV adoption.

Invited Speaker

Prof. Daniel Okafor

University of Michigan

Distribution Planning for Fast EV Growth

Prof. Okafor works on feeder modeling, resilience planning, and electrification strategies for urban and suburban networks.

Invited Speaker

Elena Park

Midwest Clean Transportation Coalition

Equity in Charging Access

Elena Park focuses on deployment policy, access, and community-centered infrastructure programs.

Tutorial Lead

Dr. Ahmed Saleh

Argonne National Laboratory

Digital Twins for Smart Charging

Dr. Saleh develops digital twin methods for forecasting, charging orchestration, and operational decision support.

Industry Panelist

Priya Nair

GridPulse Energy

Fleet Charging at Commercial Scale

Priya Nair leads deployment work for depot charging, energy management, and utility coordination with fleet operators.
