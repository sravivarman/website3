Venue

Hosted in Detroit, with transit access and nearby hotels

The conference venue was selected for walkability, transit access, and straightforward travel from the airport and regional rail connections.

Conference venue

On site

Main hall and breakouts

Keynotes, panels, and technical sessions will take place in the Fisher Center conference spaces.

On site

Accessibility

Step-free access, accessible restrooms, quiet spaces, and assistive seating will be available.

On site

Technology

High-speed Wi-Fi, projection, and poster/demo areas are included throughout the venue.

Travel and lodging

  • Detroit Metro Airport is the primary airport for domestic and international travel.
  • Rideshare, hotel shuttles, and regional transit can connect attendees to the venue.
  • A block of rooms will be reserved at nearby hotels with conference rates.
  • Local parking instructions and transit maps will be shared after registration.

A low-carbon event

  • Digital program materials will be provided to reduce printed waste.
  • Water stations and reusable materials will be prioritized onsite.
  • Food service will include plant-forward options and allergen labeling.
